About The Re-Tail Store

The Re-Tail Store is an online database that will help you find a dog for you by matching up the breed you would like with available dogs in the area that are up for adoption. We use TheDogAPI to show the kinds of breeds that exist, and then use the Petfinder API to determine if a particular breed is up for adoption in your area.
